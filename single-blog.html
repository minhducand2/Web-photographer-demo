Love is no one wants to leave

  • Travel, Lifestyle
  • 03 Comments

Yesterday I was going to sleep, there was a message from a brother in Saigon. He asked me, "Can I write about movies?"
At that time, although the sleepiness had come, I accepted it immediately. That I will write about it! "Because those are also memories of beautiful and casual twelfth grade days."

And it was also the first time I contacted film cameras.
Those days, I could never forget. When one day, the male classmate puts his old film camera in his hand. He told me to learn to take it, because he liked what he took.

Back then we were living in the countryside, not yet going to the city to study as it is now. So every time he wants to buy a film or film he will go alone for more than sixty kilometers to go to the film store, that day is Xlab in Dao Duy Tu.

Just like that for months and days, he has a lot of things to film, to remove the film, and to teach me how to shoot.
But those years were rushing away, when I kept shooting and kept burning. That day I didn't understand what the password was, what speed it was, all of it was set up by him before. As for me, I just clicked the phone. So after this, when the films received from the lab were all white, I didn't continue shooting.

Since that day did not know, Pentax k1000 "dead metering" can still be self-metering! No more shooting because he loved to travel a few dozen kilometers away just to get the wrong photos.

Then the December years also took away the beautiful time for movies.
"Pentax K1000 is still on the shelf from day to day, watching things change over time ..." Not until later, two years later, on a sunny October day, watching the camera sleep on the shelf for years, was once again moved by the stories. Once again I want to pick up the camera and go take a picture.

My photos of the first few days of shooting just don't stay in the right light. Because my camera is old, the metering is also broken, so starting with that start and the first achievements made me really happy.
Until the shots were taken back to the first sunshine, color photographs were printed on the most brilliant. The photos get the sympathy of the movie players. My photos, simply record the little things around me. The emotions were over and when I looked at it, I was inspired to continue holding the phone. "People say, love is nobody wants to leave." And when I got to know the film, I liked it, loved it, loved it, it would forever be attached, I don't want to leave. More important than ever, it is also the first love. Give a clear love story like the film in the sun.
